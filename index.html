Setup Instructions

Select your difficulty (easy mode is 30 seconds, hard mode is 7 seconds) then enter the names of one to four players. Once all players have been entered, hit "Play Game" and start popping!

Gameplay Instructions

Pop as many corona's as you can by clicking on them. You will be awarded points for each corona popped. Each round is measured by a dose of vaccine goodness, watch how much of the vaccine you have left. When the vaccine is empty time is up! Get popping!
